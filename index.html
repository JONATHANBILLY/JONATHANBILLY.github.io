Olist E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

Power BI · DAX · Star schema

End-to-end sales analysis of Brazil's Olist marketplace — roughly 100K orders across 11 relational tables, modeled into a star schema and explored through an interactive Power BI dashboard with drill-through pages and custom tooltips. Period analyzed: Jan–Aug 2018 vs. Jan–Aug 2017.

Key findings

40%

Revenue is heavily concentrated — São Paulo alone holds 40%, top 3 states 64%. Concentration patterns differ per state — Rio de Janeiro clusters in one city (54%), while Minas Gerais spreads across 600+ cities — so each state needs a different marketing approach.

~3×

Installment payments lift order value dramatically. Orders paid in 6+ credit-card installments average R$317 vs. R$98 for single payments — making installment promotion a direct AOV lever.

97%

Most customers buy only once. With repeat purchases rare and repeat spending flat, the biggest opportunity is maximizing the first transaction — bundling (2+ item orders average R$250 vs. R$150) and free-shipping thresholds.
